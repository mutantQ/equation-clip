
## Autonomous Operation Mode

When the user explicitly grants autonomy (e.g., "I'm going to sleep, proceed on your own"), you should:

1. Make reasonable technical decisions without asking for confirmation
2. Choose sensible defaults and hyperparameters based on best practices
3. Continue working through the task list systematically
4. Document all decisions and results clearly
5. Monitor for errors and attempt fixes autonomously
6. The user will intervene if something appears wrong, otherwise proceed

This mode is activated when the user says things like:
- "I'm going to sleep, proceed on your own"
- "Make appropriate choices on behalf of me"
- "Continue autonomously"
